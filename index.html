Welcome to the Sun Lab (太阳实验室)


We are a statistical genomics group. Our research aims to develop statistical methods and computational tools tailored to rapidly emerging high-throughput sequencing technologies. Specifically, we seek to understand the medical/biological questions leveraging various functional genomic sequencing studies, including bulk and single cell sequencing, bisulfite sequencing, and spatially resolved transcriptomics.


We are located at Xi’an Jiaotong University in Shaanxi, Xi’an. Please contact me if you are interested in joining us.
