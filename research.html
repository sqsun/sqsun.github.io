Research


Statistical Analysis of Single Cell Studies

Single-cell sequencing technology can study gene expression patterns at the single-cell level, allowing for more precise research on cell heterogeneity. It mainly includes the following processes: tissue disintegration to obtain a single cell suspension, cell lysis, reverse transcription of RNA into cDNA, PCR amplification, high-throughput sequencing, data analysis, etc.



Statistical Analysis of Spatially Resolved Transcriptomics Studies

Spatial transcriptome sequencing technology is able to integrate gene expression with immunochemical staining images of tissue sections of interest, so as to locate the gene expression information of cells in the tissue to the original spatial location of the tissue, so as to directly observe the differences in the tissue Differences in gene expression in functional regions.It solves the problem that bulk RNA sequencing technology is difficult to distinguish the gene expression of different cells, and the single cell transcriptome can not solve the problem of the original cell expression. It has very important application value in the identification of tissue function, tracking of developmental process, pathological detection of cancer tissue slices and combined application of molecular detection.
