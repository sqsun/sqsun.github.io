Shiquan Sun (Principal Investigator) is a Professor in Department of Biostatistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University. Before joining XJTU, he completed his postdoc training in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Michigan. He obtained his PhD degree at the Department of Automation, Xi'an Jiaotong University. He was trained as both a biostatistician and a computational biologist.


Yue Fan, is an Assistant Professor in Department of Biostatistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University. Dr. Fan received his B.S. M.S., and Ph.D. degrees from Xi'an Jiaotong University in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. Dr. Fan joined us at October 1, 2020.
