People


Shiquan Sun is a professor in Department of Epidemiology and Biostatistics, Xi'an Jiaotong University. Before joining XJTU, he completed his postdoc training in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Michigan. He obtained his PhD degree at the Department of Automation, Xi'an Jiaotong University. He was trained as both a biostatistician and a computer scientist.
